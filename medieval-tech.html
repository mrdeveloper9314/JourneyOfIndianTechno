Medieval Indian Technology

During the medieval period, India made significant advances in various fields, including astronomy, medicine, and textiles. These innovations had a profound impact on global trade and knowledge.

Astronomy

Indian astronomers like Aryabhata and Bhaskara II made groundbreaking contributions to our understanding of the cosmos, including precise calculations of the solar year and the motions of planets.

Medicine

India's medical texts, such as the Sushruta Samhita, were unparalleled in their detail and accuracy. Medieval Indian surgeons performed complex procedures like cataract surgery with remarkable skill.
